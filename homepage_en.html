Introduction

Kai Wu Education Inc. is a professional education and consulting company. The company devote themselves into servicing for students who want to attend the Art Examination. Kai Wu education has a comprehensive series of education and service, who uses standard training, personal plans for each student, exam process introduction and university-choosing consultations to build a foundation for student's future.
